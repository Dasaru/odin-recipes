Banana Bread

Delicious banana bread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 bananas, mashed

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. In a small bowl mix the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a big bowl, combine the butter, brown sugar, and 2 beaten eggs.
  4. Add the mashed bananas to the big bowl.
  5. Pour about 1/4 of the flour mixture into the big bowl and mix together.
  6. Continue to add 1/4 of the flour mixture bit by bit until everything is combined in the big bowl.
  7. Bake in banana pan for 60 minutes.
