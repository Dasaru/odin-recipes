Debbie's Amazing Apple Bread

Ingredients

This is my personal modification to Debbie's Amazing Apple Bread.

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Apples, Peels & Diced
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl mix in the wet ingredients: eggs, sugar, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and apples.
  2. In a small bowl mix in the dry ingredients: flour, salt, baking powder, and ground cinnamon.
  3. Slowly fold and mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients about 1/4 at a time.
  4. Place into a 9x5 banana bread pan and bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 45 minutes.
  5. Take the bread out, wrap with aluminum foil (to keep in moisture) and bake for another 15 minutes (for a total bake time of 60 minutes).
