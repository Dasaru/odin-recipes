Cornbread

Ingredients

Yummy cornbread.

  • 1 cup flour, sifted
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup corn meal
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. Mix wet ingredients into the dry.
  2. Bake in the oven 20-25 minutes at 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
